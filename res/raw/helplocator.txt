Locator translates network location to your specified location. Let us say you want a location called home. You can enter the location name and click on Scan button. Then have walk inside home including all the rooms. While you are walking Locator catches the network locations (not GPS locations) and groups them as your home. After finish walking press Stop Scan button. Likewise you can add several locations such as office, bus or railway stations etc. which will be useful for Profile Bot, Battery Bot and Alarm Bot to function at the right place.
