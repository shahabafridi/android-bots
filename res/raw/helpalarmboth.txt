Alarm Bot alarms when specified location has reached. You can add locations by clicking Add Alarm button and selecting one or more locations. This might be help if you travel by bus or train and want to have a nap while travelling. Just add the location and sleep. Alarm Bot will alarm you at the right place. To turn off alarm simply click on the notification bar and clear the notification. For removing alarm just click the location in this screen and click on Remove Alarm button.
