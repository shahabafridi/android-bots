Android Bots is your friendly alarm, profile and battery manager.  Alarm Bot and Profile Bot give you location based management while Battery Bot manages system settings according to the battery level. If you are new user first go to Locator for adding your preferred locations. Then setup other bots as you wish. You can see the help in options menus in each part. After finishing Bots you can start to work Bots by Pressing Start  Bots!!! and exit from the application. They will work silently in your device. Press Stop Bots for stop these Bots from working.
